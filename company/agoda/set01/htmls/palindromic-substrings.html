A string is a **palindrome** when it reads the same backward as forward. A **substring** is a contiguous sequence of characters within the string. **Example 1:** **Input:** s = "abc" **Output:** 3 **Explanation:** Three palindromic strings: "a", "b", "c". **Example 2:** **Input:** s = "aaa" **Output:** 6 **Explanation:** Six palindromic strings: "a", "a", "a", "aa", "aa", "aaa". **Constraints:** * `1 <= s.length <= 1000` * `s` consists of lowercase English letters.
