The test cases are generated so that the answer will fit in a **32-bit** integer. **Note** that the product of an array with a single element is the value of that element. **Example 1:** **Input:** nums = [2,3,-2,4] **Output:** 6 **Explanation:** [2,3] has the largest product 6. **Example 2:** **Input:** nums = [-2,0,-1] **Output:** 0 **Explanation:** The result cannot be 2, because [-2,-1] is not a subarray. **Constraints:** * `1 <= nums.length <= 2 * 104` * `-10 <= nums[i] <= 10` * The product of any subarray of `nums` is **guaranteed** to fit in a **32-bit** integer.
